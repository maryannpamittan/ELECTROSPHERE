What’s Your Learning Style?

Find out how you learn best with Kolb’s Learning Style Model—featuring four unique learner types:

Diverging

Diverging learners prefer to observe and reflect. They are imaginative and learn best through discussions and brainstorming.

Assimilating

Assimilating learners prefer logical, organized information. They learn best through lectures, readings, and analysis.

Converging

Converging learners enjoy solving problems and applying ideas. They learn best through hands-on activities and experimentation.

Accommodating

Accommodating learners rely on intuition and action. They learn best by doing, experiencing, and working with others.
